Understanding Favicons

A favicon is a small icon that represents your website, displayed in the browser tab or bookmarks.

What is a Favicon?

The term "favicon" stands for "favorites icon." It is a 16x16 or 32x32 pixel icon that appears next to the page title in a browser tab, bookmark list, or address bar, enhancing the branding of your site.

Example Favicon Implementation

Here is an example of how to add a favicon to your website:

<link rel="icon" href="favicon.ico" type="image/x-icon">

Place the favicon.ico file in your website's root directory for the browser to detect it automatically.
