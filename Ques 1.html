What is SEO?

SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is the process of improving a website's visibility on search engines like Google. It involves optimizing the content, structure, and technical aspects of a site to increase organic traffic.

Importance of SEO

SEO is crucial for businesses aiming to establish an online presence. Here are some reasons why SEO is important:

  • Increased Visibility: Helps your website appear on the first page of search results.
  • Cost-Effective: Reduces dependency on paid ads by driving organic traffic.
  • Improved User Experience: Optimizes website performance for better usability.
  • Builds Trust: Higher rankings signal credibility to users.
